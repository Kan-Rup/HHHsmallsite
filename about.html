About

I'm Kanishka K Rupasinghe. I go by the name label "Autumn-MistHare" on this site and my content channels. I first lived in Sri Lanka. In teens I moved with my family to England, Uk. After school, there I got a (2.1 Bsc Computer Science Hons) degree from Luton UoB campus.

During those years I got Schizophrenia condition/psychosis. I was later admitted to hospital/mental health ward. It was hurting, difficult and still exists, but a chemical medication dose keeps things manageable for me. I decided I have to take the help of chemical-pill to stay well and without troubles, until a better treatment/cure is found.

Work, employment was difficult at times, and sporadic. I did volunteer quite a bit in the Leicester area, such as at charity shops, Leicester Citizen's Advice, Leicester Libraries & Museums, and Leicester War Memorials. Also worked at Leicester General Hospital, in a day-clinic as HCA.

I had to come back to live in Sri Lanka with family (coronavirus pandemic times). I now live in the region I grew up in (Kandy area).

I decided to invest my time into self-employment, business, social media content and blogging. This way there is a possibility for me to build life earnings without engaging directly in the employment-work-jobseeking competition. It is a way of trying to balance hard mental health difficulties with a necessary survival effort. I am dependent on my family during this time and have less freedom.

If things go well in this effort, I can build earnings and savings, and move to a better living place. So I ask you that if you are interested in my work and see that my work has quality, please follow my work and support in whatever way you can. Competition and difficulties still exist in the area of business I work in.
